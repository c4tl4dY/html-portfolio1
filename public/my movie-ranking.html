The Best Movies According to Amanda

My Top 3 Favorite Movies

The Wizard of Oz

A beautiful fantasy film that takes place in OZ, an exaggerated fairy tale world of opposites and extremes.

The Sound of Music

Beloved musical telling the story of the Von Trapp Family Singers. I really enjoyed the breathtaking photography and memorable songs.

The Shawshank Redemption

A powerful drama that highlights the power of hope and the resiliance of the human spirit as well as the importance of friendship and human connection.
